About Us

The Cat and Dog Protection Association of Ireland provides dogs & cats rescue, rehoming and finacial aid to low income households for their pet care

Activity

Animal Fostering

Requirements
Desired Experience:
  • CDPA welcomes volunteers with all levels of experience!
  • Previous experience with cats, kittens is very welcome
  • Access to own transport to bring kitties to/from vet appointments
  • Access to a device to provide updates & respond to CDPA questions via our Facebook group
  • Hospitality/ Befriending
  • Personable Skills
