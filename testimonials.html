Our Volunteers Stories

Learn how volunteers throughout Ireland use People Willing to build stronger relationships and make the difference in the world.

I got to meet different people who made me discover the kind of profession I want to choose. I felt welcome as part of the team, not just a volunteer. Giving back to the community felt great and I was much appreciated. Volunteering has built most people self esteem and they felt motivated.
Gilberto, Atados NGO - Research

My name is Sarah.I joined this volunteer organization in 2019, to help build my resume. I have worked with them four times a week for 5hours a day. It's been a great pleasure because i have had the opportunity to meet a different set of people with other skills, which has taught me the kind of profession I want to embark on. This experience has also developed my self growth, my time management and level of exposure. I can say that it has been an amazing experience, and I can do it all over again and will definitely recommend it to anyone.
Sarah, Pares Caritas NGO - Communication

I think that each person has a Gift, in my case helping is my Gift because I sincerely enjoy helping people with fewer resources, donating clothes, toys and sometimes with some monetary contribution to some foundations that I know that are dedicated to this cause of making an adequate distribution of the things that I send. This work fills my soul, feeds my heart, makes me very happy and even more in this time that we are going through the pandemic, many people are really being very affected
Mayerlin, Together We Are Strong Company - Fundraising

My work as a volunteer began through a natural disaster in which many children were left without parents, unfortunately, I helped by recreating them by playing games, singing and having fun for that hard moment. I felt very happy being able to give smiles to the little ones
Swami Spooner, Chain of good, volunteer
