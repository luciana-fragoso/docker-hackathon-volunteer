FAQ

Why should I volunteer?

You’ve just clicked on the link and found yourself on this page. You are probably asking yourself, why should I volunteer? What’s in it for me?. The question you should be asking, though, is why shouldn’t I volunteer. Volunteering, though, is not just the act of selflessly giving your time for the benefit of others. It’s an experience. An opportunity to engage and be engaged by your local community. The chance to witness the difference ordinary people are making and their collective ability to make the extraordinary happen. Volunteering is not about sacrifice. It is about an exchange. You get back more than you give. If you are interested in volunteering, the first step in the process is to register your contact information. We will be in contact with you soon.

Where can I find information on volunteer opportunities which are available?

After registering on this platform, we are going to send the opportunities that best match your profile.

What training / support will I receive?

If you have additional support needs, this does not preclude you from volunteering. When you register with your local Volunteer Centre, please make them aware of your additional support needs. They will make sure that you are placed with an organisation that can support your needs as a volunteer.

Do I need particular experience to volunteer?

No, everyone, regardless of age can be a volunteer.

I'm under 18, can I volunteer ?

You can be a volunteer under 18 years old. But it would depend on the roles available at the time and if they are suitable for you. During your registration, you should inform your age and any restrictions. So we will show you only the programs that suit you.

I want to volunteer but I have additional support needs ?

If you have additional support needs, this does not preclude you from volunteering. When you register with your local Volunteer Centre, please make them aware of your additional support needs. They will make sure that you are placed with an organisation that can support your needs as a volunteer.

Do I need any qualifications?

No, you do not need any qualifications to be volunteering. The only thing that you should dedicate is your time. During the registration, you can show your interest and any skills that you have. So we are going to show you the only programs that suit your interest.

Will I need to be Garda vetted?

A common misconception is that everyone needs to be Garda vetted before they can volunteer. You will only need to be vetted if the volunteer role involves regular access to children or vulnerable adults. For more detailed information, please visit our Garda vetting information page. And check the Garda Vetting FAQ page on the Garda Síochana website.
