About Us

Once a volunteer, forever a volunteer

From a Bootcamp to real-world: Our organization was born as a task on tech Bootcamp. But the idea of helping the community and truly making a difference in society spoke louder, and we worked hard to make this dream come true.

We are connected with the world. Our organization was born in 2021 during the pandemic time with the purpose of connecting people.

We realize that not all people want to help in the same way. To solve this issue, we came up with the idea to create a platform to help people who are willing to contribute could find organizations ready to receive help.

Not only do we make the connection between volunteers and organization, but also we further monitor these connections. We engage them to keep up with both sides' feedback to improve our services for the future. There are many with ideas and are willing to contribute but have no way of reaching out to those in need. So we create that channel for them.

You can find out more by volunteering with us.

Our mission

Our mission is to mobilize and create a connection between people and generate positive changes in society.
